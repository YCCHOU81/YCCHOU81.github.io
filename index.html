I am a postdoc in the Department of Mathematics of Academia Sinica under the mentorship of  Y.P. Lee. My research interests are in algebraic geometry, enumerative geometry, mean field equation, and related topics. I received my PhD in Department of Mathematics at the University of Utah in May 2022 supervised by Y.P. Lee. I will be in KIAS for another postdoc on July 1st. Here is my CV.
